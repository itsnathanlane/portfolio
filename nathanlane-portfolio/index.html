The person behind

I'm Nathan Lane — founder of Lane Social Co. and an entertainment marketing strategist based in Las Vegas. I built this consultancy to bring real strategy to entertainment brands, creators, and businesses who are tired of content that looks busy but doesn't actually do anything.

Lane Social Co. has supported clients across Instagram growth, TikTok content systems, paid campaigns, and full-stack social strategy. Right now, I'm embedded full-time as the sole Social Media Strategist, Paid Ads Manager, and Creative Writer for a real estate training company with 200,000+ followers across 8 platforms — handling everything from organic content to a $167K+ Meta Ads account.

My experience runs through the industry too: PR and social media work at The B Company (a Hilton Entertainment firm), brand activations with Netflix for Squid Game, Stranger Things, and Bridgerton, and TikTok ad creation for A24. I came up through the LA Film School (B.S. in Entertainment Business), so I understand how entertainment actually operates — not just how to post about it. Every strategy I build runs on the L.A.N.E. Framework™, a step-by-step system for building a social media presence from scratch.
